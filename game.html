⚙️ How It Works

1. Start Playing 🎮

The user opens the game and starts playing for fun. There is no ad at the beginning, so the user feels comfortable.

2. Get Fully Engaged 🎯

As the game continues, the user becomes more focused. They try to score more and collect coins.

3. Feel Happy After Scoring 🏆

When the user gets a high score or more coins, they feel happy and excited. This is the best moment to show something important.

4. Show Ad at the Right Time 🛒

Now the ad appears. It does not feel annoying because the user is already enjoying the game. So, the user is more likely to click and explore the products.

💎 Why Choose This?

✔ No Annoying Ads
Users are not forced to watch ads again and again. Ads come at the right time.

✔ Fun + Marketing Together
Users enjoy playing and see the brand simultaneously. Fun, not boring.

✔ Easy to Remember
People forget normal ads, but they remember games. Brand stays in mind.

✔ Users Click by Choice
Here users are not forced. They click because they are interested.

👉 Simple Idea:
Make users enjoy first… then show the product ✨
