⚙️ How It Works (Elite Flow)

Step 1: User Visits Website 👀
A user visits the WatchElite website and enters their phone number or signs up.

Step 2: SMS Sent 📩
The website sends a promotional message like "Get 10% OFF on watches – limited time!"

Step 3: User Clicks Link 🔗
The user reads the SMS and clicks the link provided in the message.

Step 4: Purchase 💰
The user lands on the watch product page and buys the watch.

👉 This process helps our website increase sales by reaching users instantly on their mobile phones.
