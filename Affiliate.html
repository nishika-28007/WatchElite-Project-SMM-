How It Works

Affiliate marketing works through a simple but powerful system.  A brand provides a unique product link to an affiliate (website owner or influencer).  The affiliate promotes the product using content like videos, websites, or social media posts. 

When a user watches the content and clicks on the link, they are redirected to the product page.  If the user completes a purchase, the system tracks that action and the affiliate earns a commission. 

This strategy allows brands to increase sales without direct advertising, while affiliates generate income  by recommending products in a natural and engaging way.
