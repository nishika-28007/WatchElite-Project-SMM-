🚀 What exactly is PPC?

Imagine a busy mall where thousands of people pass by every hour. You have a shop, but it's on the 10th floor. How do you get people to visit? You pay for a "Fast Elevator" that takes them directly to your door.

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) is that fast elevator. It’s a digital advertising model where you pay a small fee only when someone actually "clicks" your ad and lands on your website.

Ex: If 1,000 people see your ad but nobody clicks, you pay ₹0. You only pay for the actual visitor who showed interest!

🔍 The "Search Intent" Strategy

The magic of PPC is that we reach people exactly when they are looking for us. We call this "Harvesting Intent."
