Video Strategy

CONCEPTS • EXAMPLES • CONVERSIONS

01. Creative Motion

Animation Videos

The Concept: Animation breaks the limits of reality. It uses engaging imagery to explain **complex mechanical movements** that a camera cannot capture. It’s perfect for visualising the "Invisible" soul of a watch.

Example: A 3D exploded view of a Rolex Calibre movement, showing how 200+ tiny parts work together for precision.
02. Visual Proof

Demo & Unboxing

The Concept: These videos act as a "Digital Handshake." They show the product in action, focusing on **macro textures, sound, and finish**. For a watch collector, seeing the sweep of the hand is the ultimate proof of quality.

Example: An unboxing of a Patek Philippe, capturing the crisp sound of the box opening and the sunburst dial reflecting light.
03. Legacy Building

Brand Storytelling

The Concept: Brand videos don't sell a product; they sell an **Identity**. They build awareness about your company’s mission and heritage, making the buyer feel like they are joining an elite club.

Example: A cinematic film showing the 100-year history of your boutique and its commitment to horological excellence.
04. The Solution

Explanation Videos

The Concept: Simple and direct. These videos follow a formula: **Problem + Solution**. They explain why a customer needs your service—like why buying a pre-owned Rolex from you is safer than a private seller.

Example: A video explaining your 50-point authenticity check that guarantees every watch sold is 100% genuine.
